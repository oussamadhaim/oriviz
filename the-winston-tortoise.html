    The Winston Tortoise

    Regular price €175,00
    Free shipping.

    Inspired by Sir Winston Churchill, this frame captures the essence of classic eyewear — perfectly rounded and built to last. Handcrafted from Mazzucchelli acetate, it features a sculpted bridge and solid temples that ensure comfort and durability. Confident in form, timeless in intention.

    Rims: 42mm  |  Bridge: 28mm  |  Temples: 145mm 

    We build our frames with the best materials to ensure they will last a lifetime.  Instead of using plastic or other inexpensive materials, we chose to craft our frames from Mazzucchelli Cellulose Acetate. Strong, durable and flexible acetate is considered the ultimate material for high quality glasses.


    Mazzucchelli 1849 Acetate

    Italian cellulose acetate produced with controlled polymer chains for superior density, colour stability, and long-term structural integrity.

    Premium Malleable Wirecore

    High-grade metal core with calibrated flexibility, engineered to maintain arm geometry and ensure consistent alignment under repeated stress.

    Ultra-Precision Screws

    Micro-machined stainless-steel screws with tight tolerance threads designed to minimise play, resist loosening, and enhance hinge durability.

    Prescription Lenses by ZEISS®

    All our prescription lenses are crafted exclusively with ZEISS, the world leader in optical precision. We offer single-vision, progressive, reading lenses and blue-light blocking options.

    To order your prescription glasses, simply choose your frame, click Select Lenses & Purchase, and upload or manually enter your prescription at checkout.

    Our team verifies every order for accuracy, ensuring your ZEISS lenses are made to the highest standard before being shipped directly to you.
